What is Cloudflare?

How Cloudflare Helps Websites

By sitting between users and the origin server, Cloudflare can filter malicious traffic, block common attacks, and reduce server load. At the same time, it improves performance by caching content closer to users, which results in faster page load times and a smoother browsing experience.

Why Companies Use Cloudflare

Companies use Cloudflare to protect their websites from downtime, cyber threats, and performance issues. Its services help ensure websites remain secure, available, and fast, even during traffic spikes or attempted attacks.
